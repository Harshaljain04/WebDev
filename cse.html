COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

You stream movies, play video games, work, study and scroll through Reddit on your computer.

But have you ever wondered how computers actually work or what is computer science all about? A simple computer science definition is: Computer science is the study of computers, including computational theory, hardware and software design, algorithms and the way humans interact with technology. If you're interested in a challenging and rewarding career path, you may want to consider becoming a computer scientist.
